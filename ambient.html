Integrating Ambient Displays into the Morning Flow

I'm currently working on a side project examining the way that ambient displays and IOT sensors can be integrated into people's morning routines. I'm focusing specifically on ambient displays that help users plan out their commute on public transportation.

My Role : User Research, Product Design

Team Members: Nick Stanley, Varun Gadh

Context: Independent Project, 2016

Research

I'm currently in the process of conducting user interviews with daily bus riders and a heuristic evaluation of current bus tracker phone applications.

Design

While we don't have enough data to begin designing, we intend to create prototype using Raspberry Pi's.
