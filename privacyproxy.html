Privacy Proxy

Empowering and educationg people to protect their information on smartphone applications.

I am currently working as a UX Designer for Privacy Proxy - an android application that uses crowdsourced data to detect and notify users of PII leaks from phone applications. Privacy Proxy is a research project within at the Computer Human Interaction Mobility Privacy Security Lab.

I conducted a heuristic analysis to evaluate the current interface design and propose design recommendations. I'm currently in the process of designing and conducting remote user testing sessions to further iterate the design. design.
