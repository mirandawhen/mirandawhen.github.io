Cisco Umbrella

Creating a security solution that is simple to manage and easy to deploy.

During the Summer of 2016, I interned as a UX Designer and Researcher for OpenDNS, a part Cisco. OpenDNS is a DNS resolution and cybersecurity company that was acquired by Cisco in 2015. During my summer, I worked on the product Cisco Umbrella — a new enterprise security product. I was the only intern on OpenDNS’s four-person user experience team. Because of this, I had a unique opportunity to work very closely with my team and to meaningfully contribute towards the development of Umbrella.
